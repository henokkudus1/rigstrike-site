Privacy

Last updated July 10, 2026.

The calculator stores nothing

The IFTA calculator runs entirely in your browser. The miles, gallons, and states you type never leave your device — we don't send them to a server, we don't save them, and we can't see them. Close the tab and it's gone.

The waitlist stores only your email

If you join the waitlist, we store only your email address, and we use it for one thing: to notify you when the RigStrike app launches. Your email is held with our email provider (MailerLite) for that purpose.

We don't sell your data

We never sell, rent, or trade your information. There are no advertising trackers on this site.

Unsubscribe anytime

Every email we send includes a one-click unsubscribe link. Use it and we remove your address. You can also email us to be removed at any time.
